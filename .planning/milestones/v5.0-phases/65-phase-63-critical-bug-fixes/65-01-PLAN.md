<objective>
Fix B-01 (CR-02): align `skonto.evaluateForInvoice` with the canonical Skonto basis field used by `payment.applySkontoToItem`.

Purpose: When an invoice has reverse-charge VAT or supplier withholding, `invoice.amountToPayMinor != invoice.totalMinor`. The Skonto discount must apply against the amount the buyer actually pays, not the gross invoice total — otherwise the discount overstates the eligible amount, and `evaluateForInvoice` returns a different basis than `applySkontoToItem` for the same invoice.

Output: `packages/api/src/routers/skonto.ts:280` reads `invoice.amountToPayMinor`, plus a regression test that locks the corrected behaviour against future drift.
</objective>
