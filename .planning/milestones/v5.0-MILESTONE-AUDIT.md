# v5.0 UK & Germany Expansion — Milestone Audit

**Milestone Goal:** Expand Contractor Ops to UK and German markets with contractor classification engines (IR35, Scheinselbständigkeit), EN 16931 e-invoicing (XRechnung, ZUGFeRD), UK payment infrastructure (BACS), government API integrations (HMRC, VIES), and German localization.

**Status:** `gaps_found` — 1 unsatisfied requirement (FOUND-03 i18n parity), 1 critical NEW cross-phase integration gap (I-1: Skonto BG-20 not emitted in XRechnung finalize), affecting 4 requirements transitively.

---

## Executive Summary

All 12 v5.0 phases are complete with VERIFICATION.md files. 36 of 41 requirements are fully satisfied; 1 is unsatisfied; 4 are partial due to a single cross-phase wiring defect surfaced for the first time at the milestone-audit boundary.

The phase-level work is high quality: every Prisma model, tRPC router, KoSIT validator, BACS generator, classification engine, and feature-flag gate that the requirements demanded was built and tested. The defect that this audit catches (**I-1**) is structurally invisible to per-phase verification — Phase 61 verified its generator can emit BG-20, Phase 63 verified its Skonto router computes the right amounts, and the production wiring at `packages/api/src/services/einvoice-finalize.ts` simply forgot to thread the term through. Each phase's surface area is correct in isolation; the cross-cut is broken.

The other notable gap (FOUND-03) is downstream churn: Phase 56 shipped its German message file at full parity, then Phases 63 and 64 added 32 English-only keys without DE counterparts. This is normal milestone drift, but it crosses Phase 56's invariant.
