Optimistic update pattern per UI-SPEC and Research Pattern 5:
- Use `useMutation` with `trpc.portal.updateNotificationPreference.mutationOptions`
- `onMutate`: cancel queries, save previous state, optimistically update cache
- `onError`: rollback to previous state, show sonner toast "Failed to update preference. Please try again."
- `onSettled`: invalidate queries
- Toggle saves immediately (no form submit button)

Loading state: 5 Skeleton rows (48px each).
