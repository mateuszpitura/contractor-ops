## Why three channels in parallel

| Channel | Time to first signal | Time to revenue | Compounding | Marginal cost at scale |
|---------|----------------------|-----------------|--------------|------------------------|
| **Cold email** | 1–2 weeks | 8–14 weeks | Decays — must keep sending | Linear (more sends = more cost) |
| **Content / SEO** | 4–8 weeks | 12–20 weeks | Compounds asymptotically | Sub-linear after first 6 articles |
| **Partnerships** | 4–10 weeks | 12–24 weeks | Compounds — partner refer rate grows | Near-zero marginal once integrated |

**Combined effect:** cold mail provides immediate signal (which segments × geos respond), content seeds long-term inbound, partnerships unlock CAC-zero refer channels. By M3, the three feed each other:
- Content articles strengthen cold mail credibility (link in P.S.)
- Partner co-branded content reaches partner's audience
- Cold-mail prospects who say "not now" land on retarget content drip
- Partner referrals get the same high-converting one-pager + Cal.com flow built for cold mail

Running them sequentially loses 6+ months of compounding.

## Cross-channel mechanics

### Cold-mail → content cross-pollination
- Every cold-mail step 2 includes a relevant article link in the P.S. ("Three more thoughts on this: contractor-ops.com/de/scheinselbst-pruefung-software")
- Cold-mail prospects who reply "not now" auto-enrolled in newsletter (with explicit consent)
- Cold-mail bounce list flagged in `suppression.csv` AND excluded from any future content retargeting

### Content → partnerships
- Each comparison article (`vs Deel`, `vs Remote`) pings target partner ("we wrote about your platform; happy to share before publish for accuracy review") — opens partnerships dialog
- Hub article `/compliance` becomes the canonical partner-discoverable landing page for "what is this thing"
- Partner-cobranded article one per quarter (e.g. "inFakt × contractor-ops: KSeF + classification end-to-end")

### Partnerships → cold mail
- Partner-referred warm leads skip the cold email cadence entirely — direct to Cal.com
- Partner geo focus may shift cold-mail cell priorities (if inFakt partnership signs, accelerate PL-PAR cold cell)
- Partner attribution tracked separately so we don't double-count refer-driven revenue as cold-mail revenue
