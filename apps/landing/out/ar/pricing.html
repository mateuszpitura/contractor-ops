You’ll be downgraded to the Starter plan automatically — no charges, no surprises. All your data stays intact. You can upgrade to Pro anytime to re-enable advanced features like KSeF integration and multi-step approvals.

You’re billed monthly for each active contractor in your system. If a contractor is offboarded or deactivated, they stop counting toward your bill at the next billing cycle. No charge for inactive records.
